# Vexel Group – Professional Bio & Vision Statement

## About Vexel Group
Vexel Group is a multi-domain innovation ecosystem founded by **Usama Ado Shehu**, dedicated to creating impactful solutions across technology, creative design, and sustainable agriculture. As the parent organization, Vexel Group brings together **Vexel Innovations**, **Vexel Visions**, and **Kido Farms under one umbrella, enabling the development of transformative products, services, and experiences.

## Our Founder – Usama Ado Shehu
Usama Ado Shehu is a visionary entrepreneur, researcher, and community advocate with a background in Physics (Federal University Gusau, 2023) and a Certificate in Positive Peace from the Institute for Economics and Peace (IEP). He is also a certified CCNA professional and an emerging leader in AI, tech innovation, and sustainable development.

Usama combines technical expertise, creative insight, and social impact vision to build ventures that empower businesses, communities, and individuals. His leadership extends to serving as Secretary General of the Jos Tech Community, ambassador for IEP, and a volunteer at The Earth Foundation, reflecting his commitment to technology-driven solutions for social good.

## Our Ventures

### 1. Vexel Innovations – Technology & AI Solutions
Vexel Innovations delivers cutting-edge technology solutions, including AI and ML platforms, cybersecurity systems, e-commerce tools, agri-tech software, EdTech products, and software development services. It powers the Vexel Group ecosystem with scalable, data-driven, and intelligent solutions for modern businesses.

### 2. Vexel Visions – Creative Design & Branding
Vexel Visions is a creative design company offering branding, visual identity, and innovative design solutions. It ensures that every venture and client within the Vexel ecosystem is equipped with high-impact creative communication that resonates in the market.

### 3. Kido Farms – Sustainable Agriculture
Kido Farms focuses on sustainable agriculture, producing high-quality fruits, vegetables, catfish, and grains. It integrates modern farming practices with technology to optimize productivity while promoting environmental stewardship and community empowerment.

### 4. Vexel One – Multi-Domain AI Platform
Vexel One is the flagship AI platform of Vexel Group, designed to serve offices, farms, research initiatives, security, education, and industrial operations. It provides autonomous AI agents, predictive analytics, workflow automation, IoT integration, and multi-tenant customization for businesses and organizations.
